About the Campaign

This student-led initiative raises awareness about anemia among adolescents and women. The project focuses on education, early detection, and improving nutrition knowledge through school workshops, community outreach, and digital awareness campaigns.

Research & Key Facts

What is Anemia?

Anemia occurs when the body does not have enough healthy red blood cells to carry oxygen to tissues.

Main Causes

Iron deficiency, poor diet, chronic illness, and vitamin deficiencies.

Why It Matters

Untreated anemia can affect concentration, immunity, and overall health.
